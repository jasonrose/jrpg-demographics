JRPG Demographics

Roles, ages, and sexes of playable characters in JRPGs

Quick Links:

SNESPSXPS2

Roles

Balanced characters are either very good or very bad at everything
Bruisers are typically strong but slow and bad at magic
Mages are good at magic but lacking in strength, defense, or health
Skirmishers are fast and are strong or have nontraditional combat utility
Strikers are strong and either fast or good at magic but come up short elsewhere

Ages

Children are or appear to be pre-teens
Young adults are teenaged and usually living with a caretaker, or are treated as young by others
Adults are fully grown and self-sufficient
Elderly adults are visibly old or identified as old by others
Unknown characters have either unknown or mysterious ages
